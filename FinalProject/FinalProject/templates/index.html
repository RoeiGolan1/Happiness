Happiness

Happiness is characterized by a strong feeling of joy in life, even with inner signs, such as feelings of enthusiasm, calm or hope and external signs such as a smile or laughter. Happiness is associated with good general feeling, health, security, pleasure, contentment, peace of mind and love.

Happiness is unique in that it is considered an end in itself, when the importance of the other goals that a person sets for himself during his life stems from the belief that they will ultimately give him direct or indirect happiness.

And gypsies for such purposes are: money, power (sociology), health or beauty [1], which in this context they were perceived not as goals but as means of happiness. For example, when a person works for wages he does not content himself with obtaining the money alone as a final goal for his efforts. For the most part, he targets his money to finance products or services that will improve his condition and advance him toward a sense of happiness.
